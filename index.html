Moode Art Exhibition 2022

A retrospective on the art of artist Lilian curated by Tom Smart. This exhibition features a survey of Lilian's work, including early paintings as well as her best known images that explored his experience of healthcare and abstractness. This deeply moving exhibition celebrates the artist and her belief in the power of art as a culture that generates hope, healing and inspiration.

2022.11.1(TUE) ~ 4(FRI)

@ Moode Art Gallery, Osu, Accra.

Main Program

Introduction

A brief introduction to the art exhibition by Lilian. All speakers and invited guests are introduced before the program begins

Lecture

A well prepared and powerful lecture will be delivered by professor Rowand. He dilates on the theme "Art and Culture - Its modernisation in the 21st century"

Q & A

A wonderful feedback including question and answer section for questions based on the lecture and the art exploration

Exhibition

Carefully curated arts in 2022 will be displayed for all to explore and feel the art.

Auction

Program ends in an auction of the highly expensive arts collected by Moode. Classics of photographs of amazing wildlife will also be auctioned.
