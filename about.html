Moode Art Exhibition 2022

Animals play a role in our lives from a very young age. We learn about the world through fairytales and stories that feature animals who act out human behavior, wear clothes and talk. The idea of animals in children’s literature is a recurring theme throughout this work. Young children don’t necessarily see animals as different. They see them as an extension of their human world and don’t recognize a difference. The artists attempt to regain this sense of “extension” in their work. Animals don’t feature as secondary characters but play an equal role in the visual interactions of these artworks.
